EXPERTISE

NEW ECONOMIC DOCTRINE

  • Political and state components of NED, role and functions.
  • Basic components analysis of NED of Kazakhstan: Capital, System costs and others.
  • Strategic partnership between government and business. Asian and European models.

CENTRAL ASIAN CONNECTIVITY

  • Systemic reasons hindering the development of significant and important integration projects in Central Asia.
  • Promoting integration projects for the medium term (until 2025) and long term (until 2030).
  • Creation of a Regional Coordination Hub for integration projects in Central Asia.

TRANSPORT POLICY AND CORRIDORS

  • Geopolitics of regional logistic systems: development of new corridors.
  • Development of cross-border hubs and industrial cooperation within continental economic corridors.
  • Development of special economic zones in the Republic of Kazakhstan.

TRADE POLICY

  • Foreign trade development in terms of improving the business environment, fulfilling the transit and logistics potential of Kazakhstan.
  • Construction of an effective national commodity distribution system and initial acceptance warehouses, issues of e-commerce and exchange trading development.
  • Trade and economic cooperation of Kazakhstan with other countries, barriers and obstacles to trade, simplification of trade procedures.
